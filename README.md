## What is DDC?

DDC is an open charter proposing that **human dignity is the foundation of the digital economy**.

Dark patterns â€” deceptive UI designs that manipulate users against their own interests â€” are not just bad UX. They are violations of user rights, and a long-term risk for businesses, investors, and society.

DDC provides a shared language and framework to identify, refuse, and eliminate these patterns.

---

## Core Principles

1. Human dignity is the core of the digital economy.
2. Designers and developers have the right to refuse unethical implementation.
3. Executives and capitalists bear ultimate responsibility for dignity violations.

## Why Now?

- Regulatory pressure is growing (FTC, GDPR, DSA, CCPA/CPRA).
- Companies ignoring dignity face fines, user loss, and collapse (Epic Games: $520M, Amazon Prime: FTC lawsuit).
- ESG investing is evolving â€” **DSG (Digital Dignity, Social, Governance)** is the next frontier.
